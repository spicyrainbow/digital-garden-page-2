The Trash Project

So...this is Lucy. Lucy is an artist who has lived in Guangxi, Shanghai, Canterbury, London, and now living in Chicago. Lucy loves shopping and travelling around the world. But most importantly...

Lucy LOVES collecting trash no matter where she is in the world.

However, her room is pilling up and she literally almost had to pay extra fees for storage space! We need a solution...

Lucy is struggling with throwing away her trash, but perhaps the solution to her struggles is to preserve them somewhere-and then she won't be so depressed!

So now, Lucy wants to invite you to her trash hunt!
